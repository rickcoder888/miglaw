Our Legal, Advisory & Mediation Services

MIG Lawyers & Advisors offer a diverse range of professional legal and advisory services.

Workplace Employment

Our resources offer expert guidance on employment laws, regulations, and best practices, covering contracts, workplace policies, discrimination, and harassment prevention to ensure compliance and a fair work environment.

Building & Construction

In building and construction law, clarity and diligence are paramount. Our lawyers provide essential guidance on contracts, disputes, regulations, and compliance, protecting your interests and ensuring legal adherence.

Corporate and Buisness Advisory

We provide expert legal advice on compliance, risk management, mergers, acquisitions, business structure, regulatory matters, empowering your businesses for strategic growth, compliance and success.

Residential, Retail and Commercial Leasing

We specialize in residential, retail, and commercial leasing, offering legal expertise for drafting agreements, negotiating terms, and resolving disputes to protect your interests.

Mediations

Empower resolution with our proficient mediation services. Our accredited mediators navigate conflicts impartially, delivering equitable outcomes and preserving relationships. Trust us to guide your resolution.

Planning Advice

Taxation Law Advisory

Sports Law

Intellectual Property Advice
